---
title: "Gallery"
description: "Photography and visual notebooks organised by album."
---

Each album is a folder under `content/gallery/`. Drop images + an `index.md`
to publish. Recommended ≤ 40 images per album — exceeding that triggers a
build-time warning and may slow the Netlify build. For very large
collections, split into multiple dated albums (e.g. `kyoto-2024-spring`,
`kyoto-2024-autumn`).
